Case Study

What's Effective to Address the Problem?

The most effective way to address the root causes of bay area food insecurities are by creating long term solutions that allow communities to have consistent access to what they need.

Establishing areas in urban communities where proper grocery stores, not liquor stores, can be opened
Creating urban agriculture areas in empty lots or other unused areas in neighborhoods

How is Technology Used?

Technology can help both by raising awareness about the problems but also by helping those who are addressing the problem with statistics, maps, and information

Create maps using surveys or other data to determine which neighborhoods are the worst off in accessibility to food and target those areas with grocery stores or urban agriculture
Raise social media awareness, raise money, gain volunteers

Implications

In some scenarioes, people don't listen to the communities their trying to help and end up pouring money and resources into projects that don't really solve their problems
You need to allow the communities that are actually facing the problem to give their input and ideas on how to fix it. Only having people who have no idea what it’s actually like living in their situation helping can create more problems because they don’t know how to correctly address the problems at hand.
