Initial Research

Problem Statement:

Many Oakland communities don't have proper access to fresh, healthy produce and are forced to buy unhealthy alternatives.

Need Statement:

We need more options for these communities to have access to fresh produce.

Over 250 low income neighborhoods have low access to healthy and fresh food due to rising food prices
There are barriers such as a lack of grocery stores or public transportation that make it difficult for communities to buy proper food
Microfarming can help reduce our carbon footprint, grow healthier and more nutritious plants, and help remove heavily processed foods out of diets
